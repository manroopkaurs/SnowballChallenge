Find Your Next Job

Our Idea

We notice that a lot of people struggle to find jobs, and especially when there are hundred of differnt websites where people can publish jobs. Some companies have their own websites, and others use programs such as LinkedIn to publish their jobs. Eventhough these are very good options to publish a job ad, we still find it tricky for some people to find the jobs they belong to, or fit into.
This is why we decided to start Your Future, a website that collects information from different job publishing websites and presents them all together. This will make it easier for the job searchers to quiickly find a job that fits them, and also make it easier in general for emplyers and employees too connect.
The website will also make it easy for anyone to apply a job, as it has its own appilcation system. The user creates a CV and a job appilcation which will be saved under their account. When they decide to apply for a job, they can sign into their account, and choose "Apply now" under the jobs.
The website will only be avaiable for actual jobs, and by that we mean jobs that are legal and also approved by the government in different countries. The employers who post their ads, have to present proof of their work being legal before they can proceed. The safety of user is also an important factor, which is why no one, but the employer that has recieved the job application, will have acess to the applicants information.
