Find Your Next Job

Our Idea

We notice that a lot of people struggle to find jobs, and especially when there are hundred of differnt websites where people can publish jobs. Some companies have their own websites, and others use programs such as LinkedIn to publish their jobs. Eventhough these are very good options to publish a job ad, we still find it tricky for some people to find the jobs they belong to, or fit into.
This is why we decided to start Your Future, a website that collects information from different job publishing websites and presents them all together. This will make it easier for the job searchers to quiickly find a job that fits them, and also make it easier in general for emplyers and employees too connect.
The website will also make it easy for anyone to apply a job, as it has its own appilcation system. The user creates a CV and a job appilcation which will be saved under their account. When they decide to apply for a job, they can sign into their account, and choose "Apply now" under the jobs.
The website will only be avaiable for actual jobs, and by that we mean jobs that are legal and also approved by the government in different countries. The employers who post their ads, have to present proof of their work being legal before they can proceed. The safety of user is also an important factor, which is why no one, but the employer that has recieved the job application, will have acess to the applicants information.

Website Design


When you first visit the website, you will come to the homepage. Here you will have a navbar at the top right corner where you will have two options, on for information about the website and one where you can sign in.




If you choose Sign in, you will be brought to this site where you can sign in using your email-adress and password. If you are new to the site you get a "sign up" option. By clicking on the words "Sign up", you will be taken to another form.




After choosing Sign up you will come to this site, where you can create your account. If you came to this form by mistake you will have a choice to go back to the "sign in" form at the bottom.




After signing in you will be brought to the page, this will be the users own homepage. Here the navbar consists of Find your job, where you can search for your next job, Your status, where you can check the status of your applications and Your profile where you can edit your information. By hovering over your profile picture in the top right corner, you will see a drop-down bar where your will have different options on what you wish to do with your account. There is also a settings button where you can change your password or delete your account.




If you choose Your profile, you will be able to see this site.




If you choose Edit your profile you will be lead to this site where you can edit your information. This is the information that will be shown to the employer when you apply for a job.




If you choose Your applications, you can see alle the applications you have sent, and also the date these applications where sent.




If you choose Your status you will come to this site where you will find the status of your applications. Here you can see that next to the jobs you have applied to you will see three different words pending, accepted and denied.




And the last option is Find your job, where you can choose which country you want a job in, as well as region. Further you can choose what kind of proffession you wish to work in and if you want to work parttime og fulltime. On the right side of the page you will get a list of all the relevant jobs for the user.
